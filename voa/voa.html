Resources for "$q$-Identities for parafermion theories"

This webpage contains Mathematica resources for the paper "$q$-Identities for parafermion theories" (J. Phys. A 57 (2024), no. 50, Paper No. 505201, 30 pp. arXiv:2403.03464) by Peter Bouwknegt, Shane Chern and Bolin Han.

You may find the Mathematica notebooks and PDF screenshots (in case you have no access to Mathematica) for the results in this paper.
